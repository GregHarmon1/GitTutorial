Creator's note

The target demographic of this experience are not ordinary citizens, whose personal actions have little consequence for the larger problem of waste and sustainability. The intended end-user is a policymaker or executive who has authority to create stronger mandates, subsidies or incentives around which packaging private companies will use to ship, store and track their goods. The user will begin in a bright, natural beach environment with clean sand, natural wildlife, including natural sounds to simulate animal presence. There will be signs of human existence like a beach ball and dock, establishing the peaceful intersection of humanity and natural wonder. The user will get to explore and walk around, spotting animals and listening to the natural sounds of the beach. When the user switches the scene, a future date will pop up to establish the setting for the experience of unabated consumptions effects. During this load screen, the natural ambiance soundtrack will ominously stop, and the water will change color to reflect red tides. Most of the natural foliage can still be present, but any animals are gone and trash/litter refuse covers both the land and water. The year feature will shrink to a minor HUD element, but will continue to increment as more trash assets generate, implicating the user in the ongoing pollution of our land and waterways. Success of the experience would be realized in the form of the user introducing legislation to tax unsustainable packaging or to subsidize sustainable packaging in order to correct the third-party costs caused by unsustainable packaging by empowering consumers to recycle the material instead of littering or landfilling it..
